Prediction model of quartz forming environment based on deep learning

According to the element characteristics of Al, Ti, LI, Ge and Sr contained in the quartz data samples, deep learning is used to predict the formation environment of quartz. There are nine possible forming environments for quartz, and the nine forming environments are orogenic, grisen, porphyry, granite, skarn, Carlin, IRG, pegmatite, and epithermal.
