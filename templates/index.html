Quartz forming environment prediction model optimized by PSO

According to the element characteristics of Al, Ti, LI, Ge and Sr contained in the quartz data samples, the parameters of the SVM-based quartz formation environment prediction model were optimized by PSO, which improved the prediction effect of the quartz formation environment prediction model. There are nine possible forming environments for quartz, and the nine forming environments are orogenic, grisen, porphyry, granite, skarn, Carlin, IRG, pegmatite, and epithermal.
